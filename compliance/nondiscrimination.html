Non-Discrimination Notice

Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. is committed to providing care and employment free from discrimination.

Pursuant to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and their implementing regulations, Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. does not discriminate in the provision of services and employment on the basis of race, color, national origin, disability, or age.

Our commitment

Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. complies with applicable federal and state civil rights laws and does not discriminate, exclude people, or treat them differently on the basis of race, color, national origin, age, disability, or sex. This commitment applies to admission, treatment, and participation in the programs, services, and activities of the agency, as well as to employment.

Services for people with disabilities and limited English proficiency

To ensure effective communication and equal access to care, Pioneer Home Health Services provides, free of charge:

  • Aids and services to people with disabilities to communicate effectively with us, such as qualified sign-language interpreters and written information in other formats.
  • Language assistance to people whose primary language is not English, such as qualified interpreters and information written in other languages.

If you need these services, please contact our office.

How to file a grievance

If you believe that Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. has failed to provide these services or discriminated in another way, you may file a grievance with our Civil Rights Coordinator:

You may file a grievance in person, by mail, by fax, or by email. If you need help filing a grievance, our coordinator is available to assist you.

Filing with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services

You may also file a civil rights complaint with the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, Office for Civil Rights, electronically through the Office for Civil Rights Complaint Portal, or by mail or phone. Complaint forms are available at the Office for Civil Rights website. You will not be retaliated against for filing a complaint.

This notice is provided for transparency. Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. should confirm final wording with legal counsel to meet all applicable notice requirements.
