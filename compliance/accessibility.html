Accessibility Statement

We want everyone to be able to access information about our care — online and in person.

Our commitment

Pioneer Home Health Services Inc. is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to people with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act.

Measures we take

  • Semantic, structured HTML with descriptive headings and landmarks.
  • A “skip to main content” link and full keyboard navigation.
  • Sufficient color contrast and readable, resizable text.
  • Descriptive text alternatives for meaningful images and icons.
  • Form fields with clear labels, instructions, and accessible error messages.
  • Support for reduced-motion preferences.

Alternative formats & assistance

If you have difficulty accessing any part of this website, or you need information in an alternative format — such as large print, an interpreter, or by phone — we are glad to help at no cost to you. Please contact us:

Ongoing effort

Digital accessibility is an ongoing effort. We regularly review our website and welcome your feedback. If you encounter a barrier, please let us know the page and the difficulty you experienced so we can address it promptly.

This statement reflects our good-faith commitment to accessibility and may be updated as our website evolves.
