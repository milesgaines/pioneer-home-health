Our promise

A mission rooted in dignity and comfort

Our Mission — To provide high-quality, safe, and cost-effective services to patients in the privacy and comfort of their homes.

Our Philosophy — We believe that health care is a basic human right. It must be available, coordinated, and provided in a comprehensive way, combined with other human services when appropriate. Home health care is an important part of the continuous health care system, and it will be provided in the most cost-effective way possible.

Getting started is simple

How home care begins

Anyone may request home care services — the physician, the patient, family, or friends.

1

Reach out

Call us or submit a referral. We answer questions and explain your options.

2

Assessment

Your physician and our agency staff determine eligibility with an initial assessment.

3

Plan of care

We build a personalized plan under your physician’s direction and supervision.

4

Care at home

Skilled professionals deliver care on an intermittent basis, right in your home.

Coverage & payment

Who pays for these services?

Approved services are covered 100% by Medicare and Medi-Cal. Private insurance coverage varies with individual policies. Self-pay or private pay is handled on a case-by-case basis.

We process insurance verification and eligibility, and submit claims on the patient’s behalf.

*Coverage depends on eligibility and physician certification. Contact us to verify your benefits.
